Building in Geelong

Local knowledge, on your block.

Geelong covers a lot of ground, from older homes with established gardens to brand-new estates that start as bare dirt. Established blocks often want a refresh — new paving, a deck, smarter drainage. The growth-corridor builds usually need everything: levels, retaining, fencing, turf and planting to turn a builder's pad into a finished yard. We do both, and we bring our own excavation gear so the dirt work isn't waiting on a subbie.
