Building in Torquay

Local knowledge, on your block.

Torquay blocks come with their own quirks. Coastal wind and salt are hard on cheap materials, sandy soil needs the right base prep, and the newer estates often start as a flat pad that needs shape and structure to feel like a backyard. We build for all of it — and because we're five minutes down the road, a site visit is easy and the crew's on site early.

Coastal exposure

Salt and wind off the surf — we spec hardwoods, marine-grade fixings and finishes that last.

Sandy soil

Proper base prep and compaction so paving and slabs don't move.

New estates

Flat builder pads turned into real outdoor rooms with levels, planting and screening.
