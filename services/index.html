What we build

Outdoor builds,
end to end.

Four disciplines under one roof, run by one crew and one site supervisor — across Torquay, the Surf Coast and greater Geelong. We don't sub out the hard parts.

01 — Landscape construction

Full yard transformations

The core of what we do. We take a block from bare dirt or a tired old yard to a finished outdoor space you actually live in — levels, paving, planting, the lot. Designed around how your family uses the backyard and built to handle coastal weather.

  • Paving & bluestone
  • Pool surrounds
  • Turf & planting
  • Garden construction
  • Edging & drainage
  • Irrigation

02 — Concreting & retaining

Engineered, not stacked

Driveways, paths and decorative concrete done properly — prepped, reinforced and finished so they don't crack and lift two summers in. Retaining walls are sized and engineered to the block, in concrete sleeper, timber or bluestone.

  • Driveways
  • Exposed aggregate
  • Decorative concrete
  • Concrete sleeper walls
  • Timber retaining
  • Drainage & structure
More on concreting →
03 — Decks & structures

Outdoor rooms that last

Hardwood and composite decks, pergolas and full outdoor entertaining areas — built like part of the house, finished like the inside of it. Our carpenters handle the structure, so it's square, solid and built for the coast.

  • Hardwood decking
  • Composite decking
  • Pergolas & patios
  • Outdoor kitchens
  • Feature walls
  • Fencing
More on decks →
04 — Earthmoving & excavation

Site cuts & tight access

We own our gear, so the dirt work happens on our schedule, not a subbie's. Excavators, bobcats and tippers from big to small — including machines that fit through a side gate for tight-access blocks — plus fast, clean waste removal.

  • Site cuts
  • Excavation
  • Tight-access digs
  • Bobcat services
  • Waste removal
  • Bulk earthworks
