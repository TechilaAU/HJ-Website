The work

Concreting & retaining walls, done properly.

Concrete only looks easy. Done right it's prepped, reinforced and finished so it doesn't crack and lift two summers in — done wrong it's an expensive mistake. We pour driveways, paths, exposed aggregate and decorative finishes, and we build retaining walls sized and engineered to the site in concrete sleeper, timber or bluestone, with the drainage behind them done properly.

Driveways

Reinforced, properly-jointed driveways built to take the load.

Exposed aggregate

Hard-wearing decorative finish with great grip and looks.

Decorative concrete

Coloured, stencilled and finished concrete for paths and patios.

Concrete sleeper walls

Engineered retaining sized to the block — clean and durable.

Timber retaining

Hardwood retaining where the look and budget suit.

Drainage & structure

Ag-drains and structure behind walls so they last decades.
